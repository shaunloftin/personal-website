AP 2-D Studio Art: Breadth

Project information

  • Category: Graphic design
  • Course: AP 2-D Studio Art
  • Academic year: Fall 2017 - Spring 2018
  • Project URL: https://www.behance.net/gallery/94117315/AP-2-D-Studio-Art-Breadth

As a senior capstone AP course, I completed a portfolio of 24 art pieces, 12 that are specific to a thesis statement and 12 that are not. These 12 non-thesis-specific pieces, the breadth section of my portfolio, demonstrate a range of ability using an unexpected variety of techniques and mediums.

The primary mediums I used for my breadth pieces are Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and photography. I was awarded a 5 (the highest score possible) for my AP 2-D Studio Art portfolio.
